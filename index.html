Safeguarding Policy

At Rescue Me, safeguarding is at the heart of everything we do. Our safeguarding policy is fully aligned with the UK government’s standards to ensure the safety and wellbeing of all animals in our care, as well as our staff, volunteers, and visitors. We are committed to maintaining a safe environment by implementing rigorous procedures and continuous training. Whether you are adopting an animal, volunteering, or participating in our events, you can trust that our safeguarding practices are designed to protect and support everyone involved. Learn more about our comprehensive safeguarding policy and how we uphold the highest standards of care and safety in our mission to rescue and rehome animals.
