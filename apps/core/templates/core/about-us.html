About Us

Who We Are

Rescue Me is more than just an animal rescue marketplace - we're a community dedicated to connecting loving homes with animals in need. Our platform serves as a bridge between rescue organizations, potential pet parents, and the animals who deserve a second chance at happiness.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple yet powerful: to create a world where every animal has a loving home. We achieve this by:

  • Connecting rescue organizations with potential adopters
  • Providing resources and support for new pet parents
  • Building a community of animal lovers and advocates
