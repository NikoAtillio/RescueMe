Shelter-Specific Checklists

Different rescue centers may have different requirements for adopters. When you find an animal you're interested in, check with the specific rescue center for any additional requirements they may have.

Many shelters will provide their own checklists and guidance for new adopters. We recommend following their specific advice, as they know their animals best.

If you have any questions about preparing for adoption, please contact us and we'll be happy to help.
