Our Commitment to Giving Back

As a social enterprise and Community Interest Company (CIC), Rescue Me is committed to putting the interests of animals ahead of shareholder gain. A significant portion of our revenue goes directly to supporting animal welfare charities and rescue organizations across the UK.

When you shop on our platform or adopt through one of our partner rescues, you're not just helping one animal - you're contributing to a wider mission of supporting animal welfare throughout the country.
