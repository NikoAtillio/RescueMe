Welcome to Rescue Me

The first central marketplace for animals from rescue shelters.

We are a Social Enterprise and Community Interest Company (CIC) dedicated to connecting animals with compassionate people who are ready to provide a forever home. Our platform is designed to make the adoption process seamless, informative and rewarding for both animals and adopters.

Follow our story as we build out a UK wide database of animals in need of adoption, our full web-platform is coming soon so please get in touch if you are a volunteer or wanting to be part of our story.

Happy Tails

Stories of successful adoptions through Rescue Me

Bella

After spending six months in a shelter, Bella, a gentle spaniel mix, found her forever home with the Smith family. Now, she enjoys daily walks, endless cuddles, and a big garden to play in. Her transformation from shy to social has inspired everyone who meets her.

Max

Max, a lively Labrador, was overlooked for months due to his boundless energy. When the Johnsons discovered him on Rescue Me, they knew he was the perfect fit for their active lifestyle. Max now joins them on hiking adventures and has become the family’s loyal companion.

Luna

Luna, a timid tabby cat, was rescued from a difficult situation and needed a patient adopter. Through Rescue Me, she met Emily, who gave her the time and love she needed. Luna now spends her days sunbathing on window sills and purring contentedly in Emily’s lap.

Charlie

Charlie, a senior terrier, was adopted by Tom, who was looking for a calm companion. Despite his age, Charlie quickly adapted to his new home and enjoys gentle strolls and cozy evenings by the fire. Tom says adopting Charlie was the best decision he ever made.

Daisy

Daisy, a playful rabbit, was matched with a family eager to welcome their first pet. The children adore her, and Daisy has a spacious hutch and plenty of toys. She’s become the star of many family photos and brings joy to everyone she meets.

Milo

Milo, a sweet but anxious greyhound, found his perfect match with Sarah, who was seeking a running partner. With patience and care, Milo has blossomed into a confident, affectionate dog. Together, they’ve completed their first charity fun run and are inseparable.
