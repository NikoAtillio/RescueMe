# Rescue Me

At Rescue Me, our mission is rooted in compassion and urgency. Every day, countless animals find themselves in dire situations, often through no fault of their own. We are dedicated to providing a safe haven and a second chance for these vulnerable creatures.

The Rescue Me website is built to promote safeguarding in dealing with vulnerable animals and also humans, their interaction, adoption, and how to address these important issues. 

![Mockup](https://github.com/NikoAtillio/RescueMe/blob/main/Rescue%20Me%20Preview.png))

## Features 
The website is fully functional including actionable CTA's, information points on safeguarding for both people and animals, background scroll-over hero images, zoom image boxes and embedded videos. 

This website is built for those with adoption in mind but unsure about the measures of processes and vulnerability within adoption of animals and events to attend. The events are set up so that people may raise charitable sponsorship for rescues, raise awareness of key issues, and find out more information about animal rescue and safeguarding within the process.
