Welcome to Rescue Me

We are dedicated to connecting animals with compassionate people who are ready to provide a forever home. Our platform is designed to make the adoption process seamless, informative and rewarding for both animals and adopters.

Follow our story as we build out a UK wide database of animals in need of adoption, our full web-platform is coming soon so please get in touch if you are a volunteer or wanting to be part of our story.
